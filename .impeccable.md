# Alchemist — Design Context

## Users
Practitioners of meditation / qigong / Zhang Zhuang / neidan who keep a regular daily
discipline. They value measurability and gamification, track health via wearables, and
appreciate warm East-Asian (Mists-of-Pandaria / ancient-China) aesthetics — not austere
minimalism, not gothic. Used daily on a phone (primary) and desktop (secondary), often
morning/evening as a contemplative ritual.

## Brand Personality
Warm · game-like · contemplative. A "cartoonishly-noble" RPG of self-cultivation: practices
feed a character's stats, resources (HP/Ци), perks and relics. Tone is encouraging, never
punishing (states inform, don't shame).

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme: light/warm** (parchment), derived from the calm daily-practice context.
- Built on the **RPG UI kit in `/Design`** (folder): stone frames, parchment panels, glossy
  candy buttons, colourful gems (stone ring + tinted centre), kit fills for bars, kit dial,
  real LEVEL-UP banner. Assets extracted to `assets/kit/`, wired via 9-slice (`src/kit.js`).
- Palette: parchment (#EFE6CB family), warm ink (#553A1E), jade (#3E8C60), gold (#E0A93C),
  cinnabar (#D9543B), stone greys, per-stat accent colours. Chinese han glyphs as a
  decorative-semantic layer (氣 力 定 …).
- Anti-references: dark-souls/gothic, flat austere minimalism, generic AI/SaaS look.

## Design Principles
1. **Kit-true**: every UI element corresponds to a real `/Design` kit asset (no CSS approximations of kit textures).
2. **Warm & tactile**: glossy candy buttons, parchment, stone — physical, game-like surfaces.
3. **One-tap clarity**: the day's practices and character state readable in ~3 seconds.
4. **Encouraging, not punishing**: soft, reversible states; celebrate progress (level-up, seals, particles).
5. **Mobile-first, desktop-adapted**: bottom nav on phone, stone side-rail + framed desk on wide screens.
